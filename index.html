Workflow Automation

Cutting client onboarding from 3 weeks to 3 days

A mid-size professional services firm was losing deals because their onboarding process took too long — contracts, KYC checks, document collection, and system provisioning all handled manually across four departments.

We mapped every handoff, automated the document collection flow, integrated e-signature and compliance checks, and built a real-time status dashboard for the ops team.

AI Chatbot

An AI support agent that handles 80% of tickets without human input

A growing e-commerce brand had a two-person support team drowning in repetitive tickets — order status, returns, sizing questions, and policy queries that didn't need a human to answer.

We built a context-aware AI assistant trained on their product catalogue, return policies, and order data — deployed across their website chat and WhatsApp channel.

80% of inbound tickets fully resolved without escalation
Average first response time dropped from 4h to under 30s
Support team now focused entirely on complex, high-value queries
